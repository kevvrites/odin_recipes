Lasagna

Description

How to make a good lasagna

    Ingredients
  • Olive oil
  • Italian sausage
  • Mushrooms
  • Water
    Steps
  1. Heat a large skillet and add olive oil. Swirl to coat.
  2. Add sauce, noodles, water, and rosemary
  3. Cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add cheese, ricotta, and stir.
